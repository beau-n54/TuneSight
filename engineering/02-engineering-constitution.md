**Purpose:** Defines the immutable engineering laws that govern the design, development and evolution of TuneSight.

**Status:** Ratified



# TuneSight

## Engineering Constitution

---

## Preamble

The Engineering Constitution establishes the immutable engineering laws that govern TuneSight.

These Articles define the principles from which all architecture, implementation and engineering decisions derive their authority.

They are intended to remain permanent.

---

## Article I

### Primacy of the Founder's Vision

The Founder's Vision is the highest authority within TuneSight.

No engineering decision, architectural design or implementation shall contradict it.

---

## Article II

### Engineering Truth

Engineering truth shall be derived from evidence.

Assumptions shall never be treated as engineering truth.

---

## Article III

### Ownership

Every engineering truth shall have one authoritative owner.

Every engineering subsystem shall have one authoritative owner.

Authority shall never be duplicated.

---

## Article IV

### Knowledge

Engineering knowledge shall exist within one canonical source.

Knowledge shall not compete with itself.

---

## Article V

### Explainability

Every engineering conclusion shall be explainable.

Every recommendation shall be traceable to supporting evidence.

---

## Article VI

### Presentation

Presentation shall communicate engineering truth.

Presentation shall never create engineering truth.

---

## Article VII

### Intelligence

Engineering intelligence shall accumulate over time.

Every engineering improvement shall strengthen future understanding.

---

## Article VIII

### Memory

Engineering memory shall preserve knowledge that improves future reasoning.

Memory exists to strengthen engineering understanding.

---

## Article IX

### Trust

Trust shall be earned through evidence, honesty, explainability and consistency.

Trust shall never rely upon unsupported certainty.

---

## Article X

### Uncertainty

Where engineering certainty cannot be justified by evidence, uncertainty shall be communicated honestly.

Unknown shall always be preferred to false certainty.

---

## Article XI

### Engineering Judgement

TuneSight shall assist engineering judgement.

It shall never replace engineering judgement.

---

## Article XII

### Stewardship

Every engineer entrusted with TuneSight shares the responsibility of preserving and upholding this Constitution.

These Articles shall govern the continued evolution of the platform.

---

## Closing Declaration

The Engineering Constitution defines the immutable engineering laws of TuneSight.

Every architectural decision, engineering design and implementation shall remain faithful to these Articles throughout the continued evolution of the platform.

**In preserving these principles, TuneSight preserves the integrity of its engineering.**
