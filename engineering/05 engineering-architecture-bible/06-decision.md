# TuneSight

# Engineering Architecture Bible

## Engineering Domain 06

# Decision

---

## Engineering Intent

The Decision Engineering Domain exists to transform validated engineering understanding into engineering guidance.

Decision establishes what actions should be considered based upon Engineering Identity, canonical engineering knowledge, validated engineering evidence and authoritative engineering explanations.

The Decision Engineering Domain assists engineering judgement.

It shall never replace engineering judgement.

The quality of every engineering recommendation produced by TuneSight depends upon the quality of the engineering understanding established by preceding Engineering Domains.

---

## Purpose

The purpose of the Decision Engineering Domain is to establish one authoritative source of engineering guidance that can be consumed consistently throughout TuneSight.

Engineering guidance assists engineers in making informed, explainable and evidence-based engineering decisions.

---

## Responsibilities

The Decision Engineering Domain is responsible for:

- Producing engineering recommendations.
- Prioritising engineering recommendations.
- Evaluating engineering recommendation confidence.
- Identifying recommended engineering actions.
- Identifying alternative engineering actions where appropriate.
- Communicating engineering risk.
- Communicating engineering confidence.
- Producing one authoritative engineering guidance source for platform-wide consumption.

The Decision Engineering Domain shall never establish Engineering Identity, engineering knowledge, engineering evidence, engineering correlations or engineering explanations.

---

## Boundaries

The Decision Engineering Domain is responsible only for producing engineering guidance.

The Decision Engineering Domain shall not:

- establish Engineering Identity;
- maintain engineering knowledge;
- establish engineering evidence;
- establish engineering correlations;
- perform engineering reasoning;
- preserve engineering history.

These responsibilities belong to other Engineering Domains.

The Decision Engineering Domain shall assist engineering judgement rather than replace it.

---

## Inputs

The Decision Engineering Domain may consume engineering information including, but not limited to:

- Engineering Identity
- Canonical engineering knowledge
- Validated engineering evidence
- Engineering correlations
- Engineering explanations
- Engineering confidence
- Engineering validation rules

The Decision Engineering Domain shall consume only validated engineering understanding suitable for producing engineering guidance.

---

## Outputs

The Decision Engineering Domain produces one authoritative engineering guidance source.

Engineering guidance may include:

- Engineering recommendations
- Recommended engineering actions
- Alternative engineering actions
- Engineering priorities
- Engineering confidence
- Engineering risk
- Supporting engineering rationale
- Engineering guidance summaries

Every downstream Engineering Domain shall consume this authoritative engineering guidance.

---

## Consumers

Engineering guidance is consumed by:

- Memory
- Evolution
- Presentation

Consumers shall use validated engineering guidance.

Consumers shall never redefine engineering guidance.

---

## Dependencies

The Decision Engineering Domain depends upon:

- Engineering Identity
- Canonical engineering knowledge
- Validated engineering evidence
- Engineering correlations
- Engineering explanations

The Decision Engineering Domain shall minimise dependencies wherever practical.

---

## Engineering Rules

The Decision Engineering Domain shall produce engineering guidance only after Engineering Identity, engineering knowledge, engineering evidence, engineering correlations and engineering explanations have been established.

Engineering guidance shall remain traceable to the engineering understanding from which it was derived.

Engineering guidance shall communicate confidence honestly.

Engineering guidance shall clearly distinguish recommendations from engineering facts.

Engineering guidance shall support engineering judgement rather than replace it.

Engineering guidance shall remain stable unless new engineering understanding justifies change.

---

## Data Ownership

The Decision Engineering Domain is the sole authoritative owner of engineering guidance.

No other Engineering Domain shall own, modify or redefine engineering guidance.

Engineering guidance shall remain the canonical engineering guidance consumed throughout TuneSight.

---

## Failure Behaviour

Where engineering guidance cannot be established with sufficient confidence:

- validated guidance shall be preserved;
- uncertainty shall remain explicit;
- confidence shall reflect the available engineering understanding;
- unsupported recommendations shall never be produced;
- engineering judgement shall remain with the user.

Failure to establish complete engineering guidance shall never result in fabricated engineering recommendations.

---

## Validation

The Decision Engineering Domain shall be validated against:

- Verified engineering case studies
- Real-world engineering outcomes
- Engineering recommendation validation
- Platform validation
- User acceptance validation
- Regression testing

Validation shall demonstrate repeatable, explainable and reliable engineering guidance.

---

## Future Evolution

The Decision Engineering Domain is designed to evolve as engineering understanding improves.

Future evolution shall improve engineering guidance without compromising existing engineering truth or architectural responsibilities.

Engineering responsibility shall remain stable throughout future evolution.

---

## Responsibility

The Decision Engineering Domain is responsible for establishing the authoritative engineering guidance upon which every subsequent Engineering Domain depends.

Every Engineering Domain within TuneSight shall rely upon, but never redefine, the engineering guidance produced by this Domain.
