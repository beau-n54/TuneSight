# TuneSight

# Engineering Architecture Bible

## Engineering Domain 09

# Presentation

---

## Engineering Intent

The Presentation Engineering Domain exists to communicate engineering understanding clearly, accurately and honestly.

Presentation transforms authoritative engineering outputs into information that engineers can interpret, understand and act upon.

Presentation does not create engineering truth.

Presentation communicates engineering truth produced by every preceding Engineering Domain.

The quality of engineering communication throughout TuneSight depends upon the clarity, integrity and explainability of this Domain.

---

## Purpose

The purpose of the Presentation Engineering Domain is to establish one authoritative method of communicating engineering understanding throughout TuneSight.

Presentation ensures that engineering truth remains understandable without altering its meaning.

---

## Responsibilities

The Presentation Engineering Domain is responsible for:

- Presenting Engineering Identity.
- Presenting engineering knowledge.
- Presenting engineering evidence.
- Presenting engineering correlations.
- Presenting engineering explanations.
- Presenting engineering guidance.
- Presenting engineering memory.
- Presenting engineering evolution.
- Presenting engineering confidence.
- Presenting engineering uncertainty.
- Producing one authoritative engineering presentation for user interaction.

The Presentation Engineering Domain shall never establish engineering truth, perform engineering reasoning or generate engineering guidance.

---

## Boundaries

The Presentation Engineering Domain is responsible only for communicating engineering understanding.

The Presentation Engineering Domain shall not:

- establish Engineering Identity;
- maintain engineering knowledge;
- establish engineering evidence;
- establish engineering correlations;
- perform engineering reasoning;
- generate engineering guidance;
- preserve engineering memory;
- improve engineering capability.

These responsibilities belong to other Engineering Domains.

Presentation shall faithfully communicate engineering truth without modification.

---

## Inputs

The Presentation Engineering Domain may consume engineering information including, but not limited to:

- Engineering Identity
- Canonical engineering knowledge
- Validated engineering evidence
- Engineering correlations
- Engineering explanations
- Engineering guidance
- Engineering memory
- Engineering evolution
- Engineering confidence
- Engineering uncertainty

The Presentation Engineering Domain shall consume only authoritative engineering outputs produced by the Engineering Domains.

---

## Outputs

The Presentation Engineering Domain produces one authoritative engineering presentation.

Engineering presentation may include:

- Engineering dashboards
- Engineering reports
- Engineering summaries
- Engineering visualisations
- Engineering timelines
- Engineering confidence indicators
- Engineering uncertainty indicators
- Engineering recommendations
- Engineering explanations

Presentation shall communicate engineering understanding without modifying engineering truth.

---

## Consumers

Engineering presentation is consumed by:

- Engineers
- Workshops
- Enthusiasts
- Future engineering analyses

Consumers shall interpret engineering presentation.

Consumers shall never treat presentation as the authoritative owner of engineering truth.

---

## Dependencies

The Presentation Engineering Domain depends upon every preceding Engineering Domain.

Presentation shall consume only authoritative engineering outputs.

Presentation shall never establish engineering truth independently.

---

## Engineering Rules

The Presentation Engineering Domain shall faithfully communicate engineering truth.

Presentation shall remain explainable.

Presentation shall distinguish engineering fact from engineering guidance.

Presentation shall distinguish engineering certainty from engineering confidence.

Presentation shall communicate engineering uncertainty honestly.

Presentation shall never exaggerate engineering confidence.

Presentation shall never conceal contradictory engineering information.

Presentation shall never determine, modify or create engineering truth.

---

## Data Ownership

The Presentation Engineering Domain is the sole authoritative owner of engineering presentation.

No other Engineering Domain shall own, modify or redefine engineering presentation.

Engineering presentation shall remain the canonical communication layer of TuneSight.

---

## Failure Behaviour

Where engineering information cannot be presented completely:

- validated engineering information shall be preserved;
- engineering uncertainty shall remain visible;
- engineering confidence shall remain visible;
- presentation shall favour accuracy over simplicity;
- engineering truth shall never be altered to improve presentation.

Failure to communicate complete engineering understanding shall never result in misleading engineering presentation.

---

## Validation

The Presentation Engineering Domain shall be validated against:

- Engineering accuracy
- Engineering clarity
- Explainability
- User comprehension
- Platform validation
- Regression testing

Validation shall demonstrate accurate, honest and understandable engineering communication.

---

## Future Evolution

The Presentation Engineering Domain is designed to evolve as engineering communication improves.

Future evolution shall improve engineering clarity without compromising engineering truth, architectural responsibilities or engineering governance.

Engineering responsibility shall remain stable throughout future evolution.

---

## Responsibility

The Presentation Engineering Domain is responsible for faithfully communicating the engineering truth established by every preceding Engineering Domain.

Presentation is the interface between TuneSight's engineering intelligence and the engineers who rely upon it.

Presentation shall communicate engineering understanding.

It shall never define it.
