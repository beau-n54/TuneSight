**Authority:** Derived from the Production Contract

**Purpose:** Defines the engineering execution required to verify TuneSight's engineering foundation before production implementation begins.

**Status:** Ratified




# TuneSight

## Engineering Work Package

# WP-001

## Engineering Foundation Verification

---

## Objective

Verify that TuneSight's engineering foundation is complete, internally consistent and ready for disciplined production implementation.

Successful completion of this Work Package authorises implementation to begin.

---

## Authority

This Work Package shall be executed in accordance with:

- Founder's Vision
- Engineering Manifesto
- Engineering Constitution
- Engineering Principles
- Engineering Blueprint
- Engineering Architecture Bible
- Production Contract

These documents remain the authoritative source of engineering truth.

This Work Package shall not redefine them.

---

## Scope

This Work Package verifies the engineering foundation.

It does not modify engineering governance, engineering architecture or production implementation.

---

## Engineering Domains

This Work Package applies to every Engineering Domain.

- Vehicle Identity
- Knowledge
- Evidence
- Correlation
- Explanation
- Decision
- Memory
- Evolution
- Presentation

---

## Implementation Tasks

- Verify Engineering Governance documentation.
- Verify Engineering Blueprint consistency.
- Verify Engineering Architecture Bible completeness.
- Verify Engineering Domain ownership.
- Verify Engineering Lifecycle continuity.
- Verify repository engineering structure.
- Verify Production Contract alignment.
- Verify Engineering Work Package framework.

Implementation shall not introduce production functionality.

---

## Validation

Confirm that:

- Engineering Governance is internally consistent.
- Engineering Blueprint reflects the Engineering Domains.
- Engineering Architecture Bible is complete.
- Engineering Domain responsibilities do not overlap.
- Engineering Lifecycle remains intact.
- Repository structure reflects the Engineering Blueprint.
- Production Contract remains consistent with the Engineering Governance Framework.

---

## Regression

Verify that no engineering change has compromised:

- Engineering truth
- Engineering Domain ownership
- Engineering Domain boundaries
- Engineering Lifecycle
- Architectural integrity
- Engineering explainability

---

## Acceptance Criteria

This Work Package shall be accepted only when:

- Engineering Governance is verified.
- Engineering Architecture is verified.
- Production Contract is verified.
- Engineering Work Package framework is verified.
- Repository engineering structure is verified.
- TuneSight is confirmed ready for disciplined production implementation.

---

## Out of Scope

This Work Package shall not:

- implement production code;
- modify Engineering Domains;
- introduce business functionality;
- alter engineering architecture.

---

## Completion Definition

WP-001 is complete when the engineering foundation has been independently verified and implementation is authorised to begin.

---

## Responsibility

Every engineer entrusted with TuneSight shares responsibility for preserving the verified engineering foundation established by this Work Package.

No future implementation shall compromise that foundation.

---

## Closing Declaration

WP-001 verifies the engineering foundation upon which every subsequent Engineering Work Package depends.

Every future Work Package shall reference authoritative engineering documents rather than duplicate engineering truth.
